Gifcer

This project was created at the one and only LI Hacks. The idea was conceived sometime around 3 am, and completed by 9 that same morning! The core concept is that the app translates your sentences into gifs (moving images). After entering your phrase, the Giphy API is used to automatically translate each word into the most popular gif with that tag. On top of that, this app comes with a dictionary! I really didn't want to go through all the work of manually inputting terms and gifs, so the program automatically checks if you've entered a new word in your sentence. If you have, it automatically learns the word and adds it to the dictionary!
